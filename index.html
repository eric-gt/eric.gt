Eric Glickman-Tondreau

Software Engineering Generalist doing Good Things with Good Code.

Hi, I'm Eric.

I'm a Software Developer at Curious Learning where I work with my colleagues to make reading a reality for children everywhere. We're a small team, so I wear many different hats; one day I might be programming a game that measures children's early cognitive abilities, the next I might be engineering a web application that monitors the growth of hundreds of thousands of learners around the world. I work hard to make things that feel intimate and personal while functioning robustly on a global scale. Take a look at some of my projects and feel free to send me a message if you like my work and want to know more!
